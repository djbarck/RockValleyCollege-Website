Financial Aid

Starting college can be stressful enough. We don’t want you to have to worry about how you’re going to pay for it. Yes, the FAFSA can be intimidating. But we can help with that, too. Most of our students receive some sort of financial aid and our staff will walk you and your family through the process every step of the way!

Financial Aid Course Deadlines

To be considered for Financial Aid Eligibility, you must register for each course prior to the Financial Aid course registration deadline. Please review the deadlines for each term.

For information on obtaining Financial aid, stop into our financial aid office located in the Student Center.
